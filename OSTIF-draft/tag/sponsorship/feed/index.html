Sponsorship – OSTIF.org https://ostif.org Securing Open Source for the World Thu, 23 May 2019 00:49:00 +0000 en-US hourly 1 https://ostif.org/wp-content/uploads/2021/01/ostiflogo-blue.png Sponsorship – OSTIF.org https://ostif.org 32 32 We are giving the warmest welcome to Private Internet Access for Becoming our First Platinum Sponsor https://ostif.org/we-are-giving-the-warmest-welcome-to-private-internet-access-for-becoming-our-first-platinum-sponsor/ Wed, 04 Oct 2017 13:31:01 +0000 https://ostif.org/?p=1097 Welcome to Private Internet Access for Becoming our First Platinum Sponsor!


We are welcoming Private Internet Access to the OSTIF family, as they have became our first platinum sponsors. Their commitment to free and open software has been tremendous, first funding two OpenVPN code review projects and now the OpenSSL 1.1.1 project and OSTIF as a whole.

Private Internet Access will be joining our Advisory Council and Steering Committee. They have nominated Caleb Chen who will receive regular updates from staff and will attend monthly meetings where we can discuss the direction of the organization and shape our policies to be work toward our goal of safe, secure, surveillance free systems for the world to use.

We look forward to working together toward better futures for the Internet and Computing.

Thank you from all of us at OSTIF!
