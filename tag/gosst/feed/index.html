gosst – OSTIF.org https://ostif.org Securing Open Source for the World Wed, 01 Feb 2023 18:45:25 +0000 en-US hourly 1 https://ostif.org/wp-content/uploads/2021/01/ostiflogo-blue.png gosst – OSTIF.org https://ostif.org 32 32 The OSTIF Independent Security Audit Impact Report https://ostif.org/the-ostif-independent-security-audit-impact-report/ Wed, 01 Feb 2023 18:05:41 +0000 https://ostif.org/?p=2820 Today OSTIF is thrilled to release the Independent Security Audit Impact Report. 

This report is the culmination of over a year’s worth of work that OSTIF organized thanks to funding from Google and OpenSSF. 

“I am extremely proud of this work and what OSTIF continues to accomplish. Organizations like Google, OpenSSF, and many others are eager to fund initiatives that help secure the open source ecosystem, and I firmly believe that OSTIF is one of the best outlets for doing just that. We were able to take almost $1,000,000 and turn it into significant security improvements to critical projects in one year.” 

  • Amir Montazery, Managing Director, OSTIF 

OSTIF sincerely thanks Google and OpenSSF for funding this work. More support and funding will result in more engagements and security improvements. 

For more detail, see the OpenSSF blog post.
